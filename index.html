Prompt tips for better results

Describe motion, not just appearance

Use words like slow push-in, handheld tracking shot, drone pass, macro reveal, wind movement, or light sweep.

Name the output purpose

Tell the model whether the result is for an ad creative, social post, product demo, teaser video, or concept art.

Keep constraints explicit

Ask for no unreadable text, no distorted hands, no cluttered background, and no unauthorized celebrity likeness.

Use style with restraint

Combine one camera idea, one lighting mood, and one visual style instead of stacking too many aesthetics.
